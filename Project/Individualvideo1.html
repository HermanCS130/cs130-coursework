Mogens Lykketoft on the Future of the European Union
Mogens Lykketoft has had a prominent career in Danish and international politics. In December 2002, he succeeded Poul Nyrup Rasmussen as party leader of the Social Democrats in Denmark. During the cabinet of Helle Thorning Schmidt from 2011 to 2015, Lykketoft served as Speaker of the Parliament. Between 1993 and 2001, he held positions as Finance Minister and Foreign Minister. In June 2015, he was unanimously elected President of the United Nations General Assembly beginning 15 September 2015.
