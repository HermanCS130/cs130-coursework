Iain Begg on What the Italian Budgetary Dispute Reflects
Is Italy mobilising the budgetary dispute with the European Comission to gain support at home? According to Iain Begg, the leadership of Salvini and Di Maio reflects a negative trend in Europe. Iain Begg is a Professorial Research Fellow at the European Institute, London School of Economics and Political Science and served as co-editor of the Journal of Common Market Studies, the leading academic journal focusing on the study of European integration, from 1998 to 2003.
