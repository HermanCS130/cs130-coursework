Erinch Sahan: Do Our Businesses Have the Right DNA?
Erinch Sahan is the current Chief Executive Officer of the World Fair Trade Organization. Before the WFTO, Sahan worked for 7 years at Oxfam in various leadership roles. He holds bachelor’s degrees in Finance and International Business and in Law, and a Master’s Degree International Law and International Relations.
