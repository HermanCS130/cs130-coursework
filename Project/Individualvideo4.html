Aidan Regan: Google, Twitter, Facebook and Recovering from Crisis
Aiden Regan outlines how Ireland survived the Eurozone Crisis and the role of our world’s technology giants in the process. Aidan Regan is an Assistant Professor at the School of Politics and International Relations at University College Dublin (UCD). He is also Director of the Jean Monnet Centre of Excellence in the New Political Economy of Europe at UCD’s Dublin European Institute (DEI), and Director of Graduate Master Studies at the School of Politics.
