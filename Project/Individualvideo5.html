Umar Al-Qadri: The Future Role of Islam in Europe
What is the role of Islam in Europe? Listen to Umar Al-Qadri, Head-Imam of Islamic Centre of Ireland, share his views on this polarising question. Umar Al-Qadri is Head-Imam of Islamic Centre of Ireland. He is also the Founding Chair of the Irish Muslim Peace & Integration Council, a national representative Muslim body with a presence in Dublin, Cork, Athlone, Port Laoise and Belfast.
