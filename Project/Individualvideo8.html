Tim Bale: The Two Tribes of Brexit
Tim Bale goes through the evidence of an ever-growing polarisation between the Labour Party and the Conservatives when it comes to European affairs. Tim Bale is professor of politics at Queen Mary, University of London. His main research interests are in the fields of British, European and Comparative politics, especially in relation to centre-right and conservative politics. He was the founding convenor of the Political Studies Association specialist group on Conservatives and Conservatism, which brings together leading scholars with an academic interest in this area.
