Simon Hix: Brexit and What Lies Beyond
Though it may seem rational to be pessimistic about Brexit, Professor Simon Hix believes the United Kingdom can develop a healthy relationship with the European Union going forth. Simon Hix is the Harold Laski Professor of Political Science at the London School of Economics & Political Science and a Fellow of the British Academy. He has been researching European politics for over 20 years and has published over 100 books and articles. Professor Hix is also the founder and chairman of VoteWatch Europe, an influential online EU affairs think-tank founded in London in 2009 that combines big data with political insight.
