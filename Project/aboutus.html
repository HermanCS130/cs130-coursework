About us

The Continent aims to make expert opinion the driving force behind social media platforms. Through video and podcasts, we wish to enable those with knowledge and insight to share their wisdom in a format that encourages social media dissemination. Our efforts to achieve this mission have been featured by the University of Oxford, LSE Blogs, the World Fair Trade Organization and many more.

The Continent was founded by Jesper and Jeppe Damberg alongside Herman Lange while at university. The evident lack of informed and reputable opinion online pushed them to built a platform capable of combatting disinformation by giving credible information an equally powerful reach.
